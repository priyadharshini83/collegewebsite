Placement

Campus placement or campus recruiting is a program conducted within universities or other educational institutions to provide jobs to students nearing completion of their studies. In this type of program, the educational institutions partner with corporations who wish to recruit from the student population. With the job market occupied with several competing candidates, college placement drives provide the students a chance to make the most out of the opportunities they get on-campus for securing a potential job and a robust career ahead.
